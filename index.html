What is a near-death experience (NDE)?

A near-death experience is a profound psychological event that may occur when a person is close to death or in a situation of physical or emotional crisis. Many report similar elements such as leaving the body, moving through a tunnel, encountering light, meeting deceased loved ones, and experiencing a life review.

How does the AI chat work?

Our AI assistant uses advanced natural language processing technology to understand your questions and provide meaningful responses. It's trained on thousands of NDE cases, scientific research, and philosophical texts to give you balanced, evidence-based insights.

Is the information religiously biased?

No. We take a scientific approach to understanding NDEs and consciousness. While many NDEs have spiritual elements, we present information without religious dogma or bias, focusing on documented experiences and scientific research.
