Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NDE Q&A

What is a near-death experience (NDE)?

A near-death experience is a profound psychological event that may occur when a person is close to death or in a situation of physical or emotional crisis. Many report similar elements such as leaving the body, moving through a tunnel, encountering light, meeting deceased loved ones, and experiencing a life review.

Is NDE Q&A affiliated with any religion?

No, NDE Q&A is strictly science-based and non-religious. We explore the spiritual dimensions of NDEs without religious dogma or bias, focusing on documented cases and scientific research.

What sources does your AI use to answer questions?

Our AI draws from a comprehensive database of documented NDE cases, peer-reviewed scientific literature, consciousness studies, and metaphysical research. We prioritize evidence-based information while remaining open to the subjective nature of personal experiences.

Can I share my own NDE experience?

Yes, registered users can share their personal experiences, which can help contribute to our understanding of NDEs. However, these are kept separate from our verified research database unless they undergo a verification process.
